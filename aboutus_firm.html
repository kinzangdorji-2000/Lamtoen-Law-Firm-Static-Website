Firm

The LamToen Law is a legal firm founded by Mr.Chimi Dorji, a former Assistant Judge(Drangpon Rabjam) and Registrar General of High Court of Bhutan. With an unwavering commitment to the pursuit of justice, Mr.Chimi made the decision to leave behind his distinguished career in the judiciary and established LamToen.

Our firm stands as a beacon of legal guidance in Bhutan and beyond. The name “LamToen,” meaning “guide” in Dzongkha, perfectly fits our purpose to serve as a legal guide for individuals facing a range of legal issues.

Driven by our vision to become the most trusted law firm, we are committed to delivering cost effective, high-quality, and timely legal services. Our team comprises experienced attorneys proficient in various areas of law, including civil litigation, criminal defense, corporate law, family law, and more. We offer comprehensive legal assistance across four main categories: consultation, representation, documentation, and mediation.

What distinguishes us is our founder's outstanding academic background and extensive legal experience, both domestically and internationally. With Mr. Chimi Dorji's profound expertise gained from years of service in pivotal judicial roles, our firm embodies a wealth of knowledge that informs every aspect of our practice.

At LamToen, we recognize that every client seeking our assistance is grappling with legal challenges akin to medical ailments, deserving of prompt, personalized, and compassionate attention. We are dedicated to providing representation and consulation that is not only professional but also imbued with humility and patience. Tailoring practical and effective solutions to each client's unique needs is our hallmark.

Throughout the legal journey, from initial consultation to case resolution or transaction completion, we prioritize open communication with our clients. Through various channels, we ensure that they are well-informed about their rights, options, and potential outcomes.

Furthermore, our commitment to provide services extends beyond financial gain; for promoting access to justice for all members of society, we also provide pro-bono services to indigent persons.

If you are in need of any legal assistance or representation in Bhutan or have any inquiries about our services at LamToen Law Firm, we encourage you to reach out. Our team is ready to offer sound advice with your best interests at heart.

Thank you for choosing LamToen Law.
