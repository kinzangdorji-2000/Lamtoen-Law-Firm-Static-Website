Consultation

We offer both free and paid consultation options, available in person, via video conferencing, or over the phone. Here's what you can expect from each:

Free Consultation

During a free consultation, you can:

Provide us with an overview of your legal problem, issue, or dispute - a 30,000-foot view of your case.

Receive a rough assessment of your case and preliminary advice without detailed insight into law, possible remedies, potential results, risks, and expenses involved with your legal situation; essentially, elevator advice. We'll determine if we can provide assistance or representation, or if it's best to refer your case or decline representation.

Obtain answers to generic procedural questions that do not require research.

Receive details about our practice areas and fee structures.

Please note that this consultation is limited in duration and scope and may not include in-depth analysis. Its purpose is to provide initial guidance and help you understand your options. If you have any questions or would like to schedule a paid consultation, feel free to reach out.

Paid Consultation

Schedule a paid consultation with us in advance on any legal issue related to any area of law. During the consultation, you will receive focused attention and an in-depth analysis of your case.

We will thoroughly review your case, conduct legal research if necessary, and provide detailed professional advice tailored to your circumstances.

Paid consultations may include discussions about potential legal remedies, risks, costs, and strategies for achieving your desired outcome.

Our aim is for you to leave our appointment feeling significantly better and more educated than when you first arrived, without regretting the amount you spent on your consultation fee.

Should you choose to retain our services following the paid consultation, the fee may be applied towards the overall cost of representation.
